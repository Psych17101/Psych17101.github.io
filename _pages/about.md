I am an MSc candidate in **Computational Science &amp; Engineering** at **ETH Zürich**, currently completing my master's thesis at **MIT's DeCoDe Lab** on mixed-variable Bayesian optimisation for engineering design.

My work sits where **scientific computing** meets **engineering**. I build structured, tested Python (pytest, ruff, mypy) for research and simulation, and I have contributed 3D generative models to the open-source [EngiOpt](https://github.com/IDEALLab/EngiOpt) library. Earlier, I developed multi-physics simulations at **Hitachi Energy** and led mechanical and aerospace projects spanning CAD, FEM, and composites.

This site collects my work across **software &amp; ML**, **simulation &amp; modelling**, **mechanical &amp; hardware**, and **team leadership**. Use the [projects](/projects/) page to filter by area, or see my [CV](/cv/).
